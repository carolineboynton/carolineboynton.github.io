My yoga journey

In the summer of 2019, I completed my 200-Hour Registered Yoga Teacher Training at Corepower Yoga.

Last year, at Dragonfly Hot Yoga, I taught two 60-minute Power Vinyasa flow classes weekly to a total of about 40-50 studio clients. This year, I am teaching core flow on Friday's at 12:30. My classes empower my students to discover and strengthen their mind-body connection and find an improvement in their mental and physical health. I design each yoga class with an intelligently sequenced flow set to uplifting music and assist with store merchandise sales.
