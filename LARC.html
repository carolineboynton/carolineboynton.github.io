Caroline's First Internship: Los Altos Research Center (LARC)

What is LARC?

LARC is a Silicon Valley startup that is delivering a new internet platform, “the Matrix,” with the goal of providing complete consumer privacy and security with a proprietary encryption technology to prevent identity theft, spoofing, and personal data tracking.

Caroline alpha and beta tested the platform and apps providing feedback to product management for consideration of new features and functionality. She also marketed the service and new product offerings with a team of eight peers through social media and face-to-face customer engagement. Additionally, she generated sales by registering new paid users and selling annual subscriptions to Matrix platform. If you want to see LARC's website, please visit: Los Altos Research Center
